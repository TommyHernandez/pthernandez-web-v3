This has been one of my recent reads. In my eagerness to immerse myself more in the UX world and to carry out more efficient designs and architectures, I read this book recommended by my UX colleagues. A true success, atomic design is something very interesting and when properly implemented helps us organize and understand each other between the different teams that create software. Brad narrates what atomic design is, its parts, and why we should use it. And he dedicates several chapters to implementing examples of how to apply it in a Design system.
