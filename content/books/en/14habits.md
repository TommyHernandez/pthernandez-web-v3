A short but interesting book. The author talks about habits that can make you a better developer, and contrasts each habit with powerful developers in the industry, such as Addy Osmani (Engineering Manager at Google), Daniel Buchner (Lead at Microsoft), and Manuel de la Pena (Senior Software Engineer at Elastic), so you can see how they describe them and follow them.
I'm glad to see I was already following them before reading the book, not because I'm special, but because these are things any lover of this industry already does. Highly recommended if you just finished studying or are about to.
