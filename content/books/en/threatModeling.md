I've had this book for a little over a year. And it wasn't until now that I got around to reading it. Although I have dedicated the last 5 years of my working life to being inside a cybersecurity company and working on the interface of an application to automate the threat modeling process, I must say that until now I hadn't set out to find a way to apply this in my daily work. I don't know where it will take me, but given that security is an increasingly important part of software development, this book is becoming essential.
